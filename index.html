About Us

Friends Feeding Friends

This Filipino company wants to promote the unique and delicious desserts of the Philippines. Our mission is to promote the culture and heritage of our country through our desserts. We offer a wide variety of desserts, from classic favorites to modern twists. All of our desserts are made with the finest and freshest ingredients to ensure their quality and taste. We take pride in our desserts, and we hope to share the Filipino dessert experience with everyone around the world.

Our Services

We Provide the Best Quality Services

Dessert Variations

A dessert restaurant typically offers a variety of sweet treats to choose from, ranging from ube putos and kutsintas to halo-halo and taho. The dessert menu may also include specialty drinks and coffees that pair well with the desserts.

Table Service

Most dessert restaurants offer table service, where guests are seated at tables and served their chosen desserts by a waiter or waitress. Table service may also include assistance with selecting desserts and pairing them with drinks.

Takeout and Delivery

In addition to dining in, dessert restaurants may offer takeout and delivery services. Customers can order their desserts online or over the phone and pick them up at the restaurant or have them delivered to their homes or offices. Some dessert restaurants may also offer catering services for events and parties.
